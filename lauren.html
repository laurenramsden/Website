Early Years

Born in 1997 in Guisborough and subsequently living there, I spent the first three years of my life as an only child. Though I remember next to nothing from those years, pictures and parents tell me I was a pretty happy baby.

I wanted to walk and talk as soon as I emerged from the womb, constantly babbling and attempting to stand up. Curiosity must be genetic; I wouldn't let my dad hold me unless I could see everything in the room.

All was well until August 2000. My sister was born. Used to having my parents undivided attention, this VERY demanding baby was quite a shock to the system.

I found the sound of her crying particularly distressing, and was absolutely beside myself when she was getting her vaccines- I cried more than she did!

Childhood

I did get used to my sister eventually, and found it immensley entertaining to play with her like a doll. I once painted her entire body, including her lips and her eyelids with poster paint. She enjoyed getting painted; but judging by the screams coming from the bathroom afterwards, did not enjoy having it washed off.
