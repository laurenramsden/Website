Who Am I?

I am Alessia. I was born and raised in London but had the pleasure of growing up with the mixture of Eastern and Western culture. My mother is Indian and my Father is Italian, and somehow, my parents managed to create an environment of complete harmony between these two cultures which guaranteed an exciting childhood and adolescence. For this reason, I am extremely proud of my ethnicity and feel it to be a large part of my identity.

I have a sister and a brother; both of which are older than me, definitely demonstrating the baby sister traits I can sometimes let shine. My family is extremely important to me and I think some of my best memories have been around the table, eating a good meal with family. (that's aunties, cousins, uncles included...) - indalian families are a bit too big.

My interests seem a bit simplistic, however my road to happiness simply lies within doing what I WANT to do instead of doing what I think I SHOULD want to do. Being with friends, eating good food, and laughing FORREAL FORREAL are the best things in life... for me anyways.

Stupid Anecdote

I literally just thought of this now and I didn't know what else to write so here we go. I say I love good food and I love my ethnicity but I was a bit of a stupid Italian for this moment in my life. I was making pasta and everything was going amazing (There's so much more to pasta than you think, I promise), and basically when it came to draining the water, I seemed to forget I hadn't put a colander in the sink and just poured all my pasta into the sink.
